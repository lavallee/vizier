title: Put percentages next to labels
type: principle
---

Sankey bar thickness is proportional to value, but humans read
percentages faster than they estimate widths. Append the share:

> Civic actives · 17.3%
> Evidence-grounded vote · 13.9%

Only do this for the leftmost and rightmost columns (which share a
common denominator), not intermediate stages (different denominators
invite confusion).
