title: Action-oriented conclusions
type: principle
---

If your viz is diagnostic (here's what's wrong), pair each diagnosis
with specific intervention guidance: *who should do what.*

In `info-market-voting`, every failure-mode card has a green-bordered
"What has worked elsewhere" section with 2–3 concrete interventions,
each ending in a "Who should act" line naming specific orgs (state
LWV leagues, community foundations, public libraries, PTAs).

Otherwise the viz is an interesting curio. With interventions, it's a
tool someone can take to a funder or a board meeting.
