title: Name names, cite research inline
type: principle
---

Don't collect citations in an appendix. Attach them to the specific
claim they support, in the copy:

> *Municipal incumbents win re-election 30–40 points more often than
> challengers (<cite>Trounstine 2011</cite>).*

Linked, with author-year. The full citation list goes in an expandable
"Research underpinning" section at the bottom. Readers who trust and
want detail can dig; readers who just want the finding get it.
