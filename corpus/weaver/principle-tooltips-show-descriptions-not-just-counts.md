title: Tooltips show descriptions, not just counts
type: principle
---

When hovering a node, show:
1. The node's label
2. A short description of what the category means
3. The count (in both units if you have a scale factor)

Don't make the tooltip do the heavy lifting (the glossary does that),
but don't waste the hover either. One concrete sentence > a dry count.
