title: Lede-first, methodology-last
type: principle
---

Write like journalism, not a paper. Headline and 3–5 takeaways at the
top. Method buried at the bottom in an expandable section.

Readers who want the "so what" get it in 30 seconds. Readers who need
the detail can drill down. The research-paper order ("here are our
assumptions and data sources, now let us present our findings") loses
the general reader immediately.
