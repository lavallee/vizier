title: Unresolved tensions travel forward as named items
type: principle
---

A frame doesn't have to be locked before moving to Ingest or Sketch.
It has to be **named**, including the parts still open.

Example: *"this is either a newsroom-editor coverage profile or a
civic-funder briefing; both plausible, not yet resolved — the chart
form in Sketch should be legible in both modes, or we decide then."*
The tension becomes a constraint on later stages instead of a hidden
assumption.

The anti-pattern is tensions that travel forward *implicitly* — a
designer quietly resolving them while sketching, without surfacing the
choice. Named tensions make the selection in *generate variants;
select after* deliberate.
