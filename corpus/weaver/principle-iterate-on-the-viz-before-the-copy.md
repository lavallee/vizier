title: Iterate on the viz before the copy
type: principle
---

The structure of our sankey changed four times in `info-market-voting`:

1. Five-stage (segment → mode → pathway → outcome), all combos shown — noisy
2. Collapsed pathways to primary source — better but mode was still redundant
3. Broke into main + two zooms — structure locked
4. Added percentage labels and scale notes — polish

Don't pour effort into copy before you're confident the viz structure
is right. Copy is cheap to rewrite; structure is expensive.
