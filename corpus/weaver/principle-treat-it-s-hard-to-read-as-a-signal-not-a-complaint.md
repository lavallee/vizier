title: Treat "it's hard to read" as a signal, not a complaint
type: principle
---

Every time a reader said the viz was hard to read, we found a real
structural problem — never a reader-literacy problem. "Too many pathway
nodes" meant combinatorial noise. "Zoom-ins are squished" meant the
side-by-side layout was fighting the content. Listen to readability
feedback as you would debug output.
